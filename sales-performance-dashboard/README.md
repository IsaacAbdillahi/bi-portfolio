# 📈 Sales Performance Dashboard

This project analyses transactional sales data to provide a clear, centralised view of sales performance across products, regions, and time.

The analysis uses **SQL** to clean, aggregate, and analyse sales data, with **Power BI** used to visualise trends, KPIs, and growth patterns to support data‑driven decision‑making.

---

## ❓ Business Questions Addressed

- How is overall sales performance trending over time?
- Which products contribute most to total revenue?
- Are there regions consistently underperforming?
- How does sales performance change month‑over‑month?

---

## 🧩 Business Context

Stakeholders require a clear and consistent view of sales performance to understand revenue trends, identify high‑performing products, and detect underperforming regions.

Without a centralised dashboard, it is difficult to monitor performance over time and take informed action.

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Approach

### SQL Analysis
- Cleaned and standardised raw sales data  
- Aggregated sales at a monthly level  
- Calculated month‑over‑month growth using window functions  
- Produced reporting‑ready datasets  

### Power BI Dashboard
- Modelled aggregated data for analysis  
- Built KPIs and trend visuals  
- Designed stakeholder‑focused visuals for clarity and usability

## 💡 Key Insights

- Sales show clear seasonal patterns, with stronger performance toward year‑end  
- A small number of products contribute a significant share of total revenue  
- Certain regions consistently underperform relative to others  
- Month‑over‑month growth highlights periods of volatility useful for early issue detection  

---

## ✅ Recommendations

- Focus commercial efforts on high‑performing products during peak periods  
- Investigate structural or operational challenges in underperforming regions  
- Use month‑over‑month growth tracking as an early warning indicator for performance changes
