# 🧠 Customer Segmentation & Value Analysis

This project analyses customer-level sales data to understand how revenue is distributed across the customer base and to identify high-value customer segments.

The analysis uses **SQL** to aggregate, rank, and segment customers based on revenue contribution, with **Power BI** used to explore insights and support business decision‑making.

---

## ❓ Business Questions Addressed

- How is revenue distributed across the customer base?
- Which customers contribute the highest share of total revenue?
- Can customers be grouped into meaningful value-based segments?
- Where is customer value most concentrated?

---

## 🧩 Business Context

Understanding customer value distribution is critical for prioritising retention, targeting high‑value customers, and optimising commercial efforts.

Without segmentation, stakeholders lack visibility into which customers drive the majority of revenue and where focused engagement strategies could deliver the greatest impact.

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Approach

### SQL Analysis
- Aggregated customer-level revenue
- Ranked customers by revenue contribution
- Applied value‑based segmentation logic
- Produced reporting‑ready datasets for analysis

### Power BI Analysis
- Visualised revenue concentration across customer segments
- Explored segment-level performance and contribution
- Designed visuals to support stakeholder decision‑making

## 💡 Key Insights

- A small proportion of customers generates a disproportionately large share of total revenue
- High‑value customers can be grouped into clear, distinct segments
- Revenue concentration highlights opportunities for targeted retention and engagement strategies

---

## ✅ Recommendations

- Focus retention and engagement efforts on high‑value customer segments
- Develop tailored strategies for customers with high revenue contribution
- Use customer segmentation to inform commercial and marketing decision‑making
